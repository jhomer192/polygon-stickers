Add Polygons to your iPhone

30 seconds, no App Store. Polygons is a web app — Safari saves it to your Home Screen and it opens full-screen like any other app. Free forever.

1. Install the app (one time)

  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome — Safari is the only browser that can add to Home Screen on iOS).
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari.
  3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
  4. Tap Add. The Polygons icon appears on your Home Screen.

From now on, tap the icon to open the app full-screen.

2. Make a sticker

  1. Open Polygons. Set the shape, sides, color, and rotation you want.
  2. Tap Save / Share.
  3. iOS opens the share sheet → tap Save Image (saves to Photos). Or Copy the polygon and paste it straight into Messages.

3. Add it to your iPhone sticker collection

iOS 17+ lifts subjects from any photo into a real sticker — no extra app needed.

  1. Open Photos and find the polygon you just saved.
  2. Long-press directly on the polygon shape (1 second). iOS lifts the subject — you'll see a shimmer around the edge.
  3. A menu pops up. Tap “Add Sticker”.
  4. That's it. The sticker now lives in your system sticker drawer — available in iMessage, Mail, Notes, anywhere stickers go.

Tip: the larger your polygon fills the canvas, the better iOS isolates it as a subject. If iOS struggles to pick it up, try adding a stroke or drop shadow for stronger edges.

Using your stickers in Messages

  1. Open Messages, tap the + next to the text box.
  2. Tap Stickers.
  3. Your polygons appear under “Your Stickers”. Tap to send, or drag onto an existing message bubble.

Why does this app work without the App Store?

Polygons is a Progressive Web App (PWA). It's a normal website that Safari can save to your Home Screen. When you tap the icon, it opens full-screen with no browser chrome — indistinguishable from a native app for what this does. It runs entirely on your phone; nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored on a server.
