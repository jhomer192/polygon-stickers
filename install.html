Add Polygons to your iPhone

30 seconds, no App Store. Polygons is a web app — Safari saves it to your Home Screen and it opens full-screen like any other app. Free forever.

1. Install the app (one time)

  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome — Safari is the only browser that can add to Home Screen on iOS).
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari.
  3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
  4. Tap Add. The Polygons icon appears on your Home Screen.

From now on, tap the icon to open the app full-screen.

2. Make a sticker

Two ways: design a polygon from scratch, or turn any photo / screenshot / meme into a sticker.

Option A — Polygon from scratch

  1. Open Polygons. Tap Regular or Star.
  2. Set sides, color, rotation, stroke, effects.
  3. Tap Save / Share → Save Image.

Option B — Photo → Sticker

Got a chess.com "Blunder" badge, a screenshot of a friend's text, or any image you want to drop into iMessage as a sticker? This is the mode for it.

  1. Tap the Photo tab.
  2. Tap Choose photo and pick any image — screenshot, photo, meme, anything.
  3. Erase the background. You have three tools:
    • Magic wand: tap any pixel and it removes everything matching that color. Great for flat backgrounds like white, app screenshots, or solid colors. Adjust tolerance higher to grab more similar pixels per tap.
    • Eraser: drag to manually wipe pixels. Use this to clean up edges the wand misses.
    • Restore: drag to bring back pixels you erased by mistake.
  4. (Optional) Tap Auto-remove bg for AI subject isolation. First tap downloads a ~30 MB model to your phone — after that it runs offline. Best for photos with people, animals, or objects against a busy background.
  5. (Optional) Tap Crop to subject to tighten the sticker around what's left.
  6. Tap Save / Share → Save Image.

Everything happens on your phone. Photos never leave the device. There's no upload, no server, no account.

3. Add it to your iPhone sticker collection

iOS 17+ lifts subjects from any photo into a real sticker — no extra app needed.

  1. Open Photos and find the polygon you just saved.
  2. Long-press directly on the polygon shape (1 second). iOS lifts the subject — you'll see a shimmer around the edge.
  3. A menu pops up. Tap “Add Sticker”.
  4. That's it. The sticker now lives in your system sticker drawer — available in iMessage, Mail, Notes, anywhere stickers go.

Tip: the larger your polygon fills the canvas, the better iOS isolates it as a subject. If iOS struggles to pick it up, try adding a stroke or drop shadow for stronger edges.

Using your stickers in Messages

  1. Open Messages, tap the + next to the text box.
  2. Tap Stickers.
  3. Your stickers appear under “Your Stickers”. Tap to send, or long-press and drag the sticker onto an existing message bubble to attach it to a specific message — perfect for slapping a "Blunder" sticker onto your friend's horrendous take.

Why does this app work without the App Store?

Polygons is a Progressive Web App (PWA). It's a normal website that Safari can save to your Home Screen. When you tap the icon, it opens full-screen with no browser chrome — indistinguishable from a native app for what this does. It runs entirely on your phone; nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored on a server.
